# Independent Events in Union Problems

Given: A and B are independent.

Need:  P(A ∪ B)

Workflow:  P(A ∪ B) = P(A) + P(B) − P(A ∩ B)

Since A and B are independent: P(A ∩ B) = P(A)P(B)

Therefore:  P(A ∪ B) = P(A) + P(B) − P(A)P(B)

---

Common Trap

Independent ≠ Disjoint

Disjoint (Mutually Exclusive):  P(A ∩ B) = 0

Independent:  P(A ∩ B) = P(A)P(B)

Two nontrivial events cannot be both independent and disjoint.
